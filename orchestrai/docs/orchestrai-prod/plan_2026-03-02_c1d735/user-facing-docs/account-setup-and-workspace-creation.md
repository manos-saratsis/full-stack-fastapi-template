# Getting Started: Create Your OrchestrAI Account & Set Up Your Workspace

Welcome to OrchestrAI! This guide takes you from zero to a fully configured workspace — step by step. By the end, you'll have an active account, a personalised profile, and your team ready to go.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Visit the OrchestrAI sign-up page to get started. You'll see a simple form asking for four pieces of information:

| Field | What to enter |
|---|---|
| **Full Name** | Your first and last name (minimum 3 characters) |
| **Email** | A valid email address you have access to |
| **Password** | A secure password of your choice |
| **Confirm Password** | Re-type your password to confirm it matches |

Once all fields are filled in correctly, click **Sign Up**.

> **Already have an account?** Click the **Log In** link at the bottom of the form to go straight to the login page.

---

## Step 2: Log In for the First Time

After signing up, you'll be taken to the **Log In** page. Enter the email address and password you just created, then click **Log In**.

OrchestrAI will verify your details and bring you straight into your workspace dashboard.

> **Forgot your password?** Click the **Forgot Password?** link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it securely via email.

---

## Step 3: What Is a Workspace?

Your **workspace** is your personalised home inside OrchestrAI — the shared environment where you and your team do all your work. Think of it as a dedicated hub that keeps everything organised in one place: your projects, your team members, and your activity.

When you log in for the first time, your workspace is automatically created and ready for you. There is nothing extra to configure — just log in and you're in.

---

## Step 4: Your Dashboard — First Look

After logging in, you land on your **Dashboard**. This is the central starting point for everything in OrchestrAI. You'll see a personalised greeting:

> *"Hi, [Your Name] 👋🏼"*

On the left-hand side of the screen, you'll find the **sidebar** — your main navigation menu. It contains:

| Sidebar Item | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Your home page — a summary overview |
| **Items** | The core workspace for your projects and content |
| **Settings** | Manage your personal account preferences |
| **Admin** | Manage team members *(visible to administrators only)* |

---

## Step 5: Complete Your Profile

Before inviting teammates, it's a good idea to complete your profile so others can identify you easily.

1. Click on **Settings** in the left sidebar.
2. You'll land on the **My Profile** tab, where you can view and update your:
   - **Full Name**
   - **Email Address**
3. Click **Edit**, make your changes, then click **Save**.
   To cancel without saving, click **Cancel**.

The Settings page has four tabs:

| Tab | What you can do there |
|---|---|
| **My Profile** | Update your name and email address |
| **Password** | Change your current password |
| **Appearance** | Personalise how OrchestrAI looks for you |
| **Danger Zone** | Delete your account *(available to standard users only)* |

> **Note for Administrators:** The Danger Zone tab is hidden for Superuser accounts, as administrator accounts are managed differently.

---

## Step 6: Invite Team Members

Once your workspace is set up, you can bring your team in. Team management is available to **administrators (Superusers)** via the **Admin** section in the left sidebar.

### How to add a team member

1. Click **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Click the **Add User** button at the top of the page.
3. Fill in the new member's details — their name, email, password, and role.
4. Click **Save**. The new member will now appear in your team list and can log in immediately.

### Viewing your team

The Admin page shows a table of everyone in your workspace:

| Column | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Full Name** | The member's display name |
| **Email** | Their login email address |
| **Role** | **User** or **Superuser** |
| **Status** | **Active** or **Inactive** |
| **Actions** | Edit or remove the member |

Your own account is marked with a **"You"** badge. You cannot edit or remove yourself from this view.

> **Don't see the Admin section?** Only Superuser accounts can manage team members. Ask your administrator to grant you elevated access.

---

## Step 7: Understanding Roles

OrchestrAI has two roles that control what each person can do:

| Role | What they can do |
|---|---|
| **User** | Access the Dashboard and Items; manage their own profile and password |
| **Superuser (Admin)** | Everything a User can do, plus add, edit, deactivate, and remove other team members |

When adding a new team member, an administrator chooses which role to assign. Roles can be updated at any time from the Admin page.

## Troubleshooting

**The Sign Up button isn't responding.**
Make sure all fields are filled in correctly. Your full name must be at least 3 characters long, your email must be in a valid format (e.g. `name@example.com`), and both password fields must match exactly.

**I can't log in.**
Double-check you're using the same email address you registered with. If you've forgotten your password, use the **Forgot Password?** link on the login page to reset it.

**My name shows as "N/A" in the team list.**
Navigate to **Settings → My Profile**, click **Edit**, enter your full name, and click **Save**.

**I don't see the Admin section in the sidebar.**
The Admin area is only visible to Superuser accounts. Contact your workspace administrator to have your role upgraded.

**The team member I added can't log in.**
Verify that their account status shows as **Active** in the Admin table. If it shows **Inactive**, click the **Actions** menu for that user and activate their account.
