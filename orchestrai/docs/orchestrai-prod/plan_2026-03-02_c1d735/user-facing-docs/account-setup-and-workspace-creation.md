# Getting Started: Create Your OrchestrAI Account & Workspace

Welcome to OrchestrAI! This guide walks you through everything you need to do — from creating your account to landing on your dashboard — so you can start working with your team right away.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account

To get started, navigate to the **Sign Up** page.

You will be asked to fill in three pieces of information:

| Field | What to enter |
|---|---|
| **Full Name** | Your first and last name (at least 3 characters) |
| **Email** | A valid email address you have access to |
| **Password** | A secure password of your choice |
| **Confirm Password** | Re-enter your password to confirm it matches |

Once all fields are filled in, click the **Sign Up** button.

> **Already have an account?** Click the **Log In** link at the bottom of the Sign Up form to go directly to the login page.

---

## Step 2: Log In to OrchestrAI

After signing up, you are automatically taken to the **Log In** page.

Enter your:
- **Email address**
- **Password**

Then click **Log In**.

> **Forgot your password?** Click the **Forgot Password?** link on the login page. You will be guided through a secure password reset process using your email address.

Once you log in successfully, OrchestrAI brings you straight to your personal **Dashboard**.

---

## Step 3: Your Dashboard — What You'll See First

After logging in, you land on your Dashboard. The page greets you by name:

> *"Hi, [Your Name] 👋🏼 — Welcome back, nice to see you again!"*

Your Dashboard is the central home base of your OrchestrAI experience. From here you can navigate to all features using the **sidebar** on the left side of the screen.

The sidebar includes:
- **Dashboard** — your home view
- **Items** — the main workspace for your work
- **Settings** — your personal account preferences
- **Admin** *(visible to administrators only)* — team and user management

---

## Step 4: Set Up Your Profile

Before inviting team members, take a moment to complete your profile. Navigate to **Settings** using the left sidebar.

Under the **My Profile** tab, you can view and update:
- **Full Name**
- **Email Address**

To make changes, click the **Edit** button, update your details, and click **Save**. To discard any changes, click **Cancel**.

The Settings page also includes:
- **Password** tab — change your current password
- **Appearance** tab — personalise the look and feel of the application
- **Danger Zone** tab — options for account deletion *(regular users only)*

---

## Step 5: Manage Your Team (Administrators)

If you have been given **administrator** (Superuser) access, you will see an **Admin** section in the left sidebar. This is the area where you manage everyone who has access to your OrchestrAI workspace.

### Adding a Team Member

1. Navigate to the **Admin** page from the sidebar.
2. Click the **Add User** button at the top of the page.
3. Fill in the new team member's details.
4. Confirm to send them access.

### Viewing Your Team

The Admin page displays a table of all users in your workspace, showing:

| Column | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Full Name** | The member's display name |
| **Email** | Their login email address |
| **Role** | Either *User* or *Superuser* |
| **Status** | Whether the account is *Active* or *Inactive* |
| **Actions** | Options to edit or remove the member |

Your own account is highlighted with a **"You"** badge so you can easily identify it. You cannot edit or remove your own account from this view.

> **Not seeing the Admin section?** Only Superuser accounts have access to team management. Contact your administrator to request elevated permissions.

---

## Step 6: Understanding Roles

OrchestrAI has two user roles:

| Role | What they can do |
|---|---|
| **User** | Access the Dashboard and Items; manage their own profile and password |
| **Superuser (Admin)** | Everything a User can do, plus add, edit, and deactivate other team members |

## Quick Troubleshooting

**I can't log in.**
Double-check that you are using the email address you registered with. If you have forgotten your password, use the **Forgot Password?** link on the login page.

**My name shows as "N/A" on the Dashboard.**
Navigate to **Settings → My Profile**, click **Edit**, enter your full name, and click **Save**.

**I don't see the Admin section in the sidebar.**
The Admin area is only visible to Superuser accounts. Ask your workspace administrator to upgrade your role.

**The Sign Up button isn't working.**
Ensure all fields are filled in correctly — your full name must be at least 3 characters, your email must be a valid format, and both password fields must match.
